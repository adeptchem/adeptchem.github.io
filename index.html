About AdeptCoat

Adept Coat provides medical device coatings to the healthcare product manufacturers

AdeptCoat

Adept Coat is provider of end to end coating solutions and services for medical device industries. Adept coat develop s custom coating solutions that are bio derived and provide highly lubricious coatings for medical devices.

Adept coat also provides coating services and expert solutions to medical start-ups.

Product Featuress

Application

Adept coatings are applied as coating over catheters used in Cardiovascular and Neurovascular field.

Performance

Adept coatings are applied to medical devics to render imperable suface characteristics in turn providing highly lubricious surface which is hydrophilic and effortlessly passes through vasculature without damaging the vessel walls and eases positoning of the device.

Services

We provide coating solutions, help with process development for coating, testing, verification etc.

Coatings Development

Adept coat helps choosing appropriate coating solution and formulation as per your application and surface characteristics of the medical device.


Optimization and Testing

The rugedness and efficacy of the new coating is tested by varying various conditions and parameters for coating.


The Coatings Development Process

Post the coating process development and validation the important step is scale-up process. Adept coat provides solutions for manufacturing process and scale-up to provide reproducible results keeping GMP grade in mind.

Creating a Coating Process.

Adept coatworks in collabaration with the client to help decide the proper parameters and equipment for the coating process.

Manufacturing Process Support

Adept coat provides constant consultancy to ensure the process tuns smoothly over the periods of time.
